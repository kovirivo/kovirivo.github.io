STUDY ZONE at KCLS

About

Study Zone at the Boulevard Park Library is open to ANYONE who is in need of tutoring. Whether you need asssistance with algebra, reading, or any other subject, we will always try our best to help you. We provide materials such as paper, rulers, dictionaries, and much more. We can also print out extra worksheets or homework for you to practice on. If you have trouble communicating with us, let us know and we will provide a translator. Don't be shy and come on over!

ABOUT

This website was created to help potential Study Zone visitors and volunteers understand what Study Zone is. It is also designed to help advertise Study Zone. The website contains two infographics that try to be as accessible for as many people as possible. Certain aspects of Study Zone were highlighted based on its targeted demographic. Study Zone being free is not always known by many patrons. With these certain aspects quickly and highlighted, many are able to quickly understand what Study Zone is. The website was also created to recruit tutors. Many do not realize the benefits of helping out community members and how much they are needed. Overall, Study Zone needs to be seen by more people, as it is a valuable asset to the community.
